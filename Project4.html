Devotion:-

We always want the best for ourselves right? So why not for the Lord of universe? We always try to be the best versions of ourselves so that when we meet him we can truly serve him at our best!

Offerings:-

In the Bhagwat Geeta, the lord says, "Patram, Pushpam, Falam, Toyam", means, if a devotee offers any thing as small as a leaf ( tulsi ), or a flower, a fruit or even some water with love and devotion, the Lord accepts it.
